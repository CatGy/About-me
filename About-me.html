About me

What can I say about me! My friends and family say I'm friendly, kind and funny. I am a reliable person, have a great sens of humour, open-minded and do not hesitate to help others that are in need. I am down to earth and usually open to discover new things (Cultures, food, places...). I do enjoy playing different types of games like Pools/billiard, Cards, Dominoes, Checkers, monopoly...). I am a loner at times and I do enjoy that time to relax, focus on me and explore.
